Privacy Policy

HRIS Asuka | Effective Date: December 15, 2024

PT Asuka ("we", "our", or "us") operates the HRIS Asuka mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Employee ID and username
  • Full name and position
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted)

1.2 Location Data

We collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) when you:

  • Clock in or clock out for attendance
  • Location data is collected only at the moment of attendance submission
  • We do not track your location in the background

1.3 Biometric Data

We use facial recognition technology to verify your identity during attendance:

  • Facial data is processed on-device using Google ML Kit
  • Facial images are captured temporarily for verification purposes
  • Facial data is not stored permanently on our servers
  • Facial recognition is used solely for attendance verification

1.4 Device Information

We may collect device information including:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Network information (WiFi SSID for office verification)

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
User authentication Username, password, employee ID
Attendance recording Location data, timestamp
Identity verification Facial recognition data
Attendance validation GPS location, WiFi network
HR management Employee information, attendance records

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Personal data is stored on secure servers located in Indonesia
  • Local device storage uses encrypted storage mechanisms
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform-specific secure storage

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted local storage for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within the Organization: HR department and authorized personnel for employment purposes
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that assist in app functionality (e.g., Google ML Kit for face detection)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose
Google ML Kit Face detection for identity verification
Google Play Services App distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies. Please refer to Google Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact your HR department or IT support.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as:

  • You are an active employee
  • Required by employment law and regulations
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes

Attendance records are retained according to Indonesian labor law requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for use by employees of PT Asuka only. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending notification through company communication channels
